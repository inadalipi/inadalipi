# Hi, I’m Ina 👋

I’m the Founder & CEO of **MATRY**, the AI Cofounder for solo founders.

MATRY helps founders turn scattered goals, client work, follow-ups, and ideas into a clear execution system, so they always know what to do next.

## What I’m building

**MATRY** is an AI Cofounder designed for solo founders, freelancers and online service providers building their businesses alone.

The product includes:

- Business assessment
- Personalized plan
- AI Copilot with business context
- Command Center for daily priorities
- Execution support for founders who need clarity, not another blank workspace

## Current focus

Right now I’m working on:

- Improving the MATRY onboarding and assessment flow
- Refining the Personalized Plan experience
- Building the Command Center
- Testing with early users and founding members
- Reviewing PRs, QA, product logic, user feedback, and backend milestones
- Building the foundation for a human-centered AI company

## My approach to AI

I believe the future of AI is not replacement.

It is human amplification.

My work focuses on human-centered AI, founder execution, emotional intelligence, and building tools that help people carry less, decide better, and move with more clarity.
